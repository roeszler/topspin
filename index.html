The Rules

TopTails is a variation of the iconic and traditional Australian gambling game 'Two-up'.
The basic format of the game is:

  • Reach 500 points before Kev D Koala does to win the game.
  • Betting and winning Two Heads (HH) or a combination of a Head and a Tail (HT) will win 2x the wagered bet
  • Betting on spinning up TwoTails (TT) will win four times the wagered bet (x4); However...
    • ... if any other combination is thrown, Kev wins SIX TIMES (x6) the amount you wagered.
  • If you loose any other the spin, the wagered bet is forefitied to Kev.
  • If you empty the bank and/or Your or Kev's Score gets to 500 points, the game is ended.
  • You can't bet more than the amount you have in the bank.
